722 Capistrano Place

722 Capistrano Place is a great destination for families wishing to stay in the South Mission Beach area.

Owned by Martha and Robert for decades this is the a great safe place to take your family for a vacation.

A stones throw from the bay and even closer to the ocean the beaches offer swing for families of all ages and sizes.
